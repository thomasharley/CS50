Hello, I'm Thomas!

Electronic Systems Engineer based in Edinburgh, Scotland.

Welcome to my personal homepage.

I hold a First Class Master's Degree (MEng) in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the University of Edinburgh.

From May to September of 2021 I worked at the global semiconductor firm Analog Devices as a System Engineering Intern. In 2022, I completed a 6 month Master's placement at Analog Devices, working on the next generation of DC Metering system level products targeting EV charging applications.

I am now working at Analog Devices full time as a Systems Engineer in the Energy and Automotive Group. My role largely consists of the design and test of evaluation boards and reference designs for new silicon products. I am also involved in the embedded software development for the chips and in the preparation of detailed technical documentation, user guides and overall collateral for customers.

Specific Interests include: PCB design and layout, digital system design, FPGA, System Verilog and embedded software development. If you would like to receive a copy of my CV reach out to me on LinkedIn!
